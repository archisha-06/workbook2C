Adding a landing page
In Week 7, I started building the home/landing page for my Workbook 2C. I decided to stick with the cyberpunk-tech aesthetic I used in my previous workbook, but this time I wanted to push it further and make it feel more refined—less chaotic, more intentional. I edited a PNG of a computer and wrapped text around it, just to give it that layered, glitchy feel. Then I had the idea to actually place my links inside the screen of the computer, which felt like a nice little interactive touch that fit the theme. It sounded simple enough in my head, but getting the placement right took a fair bit of fiddling—things kept shifting or looking slightly off. After some trial and error (and a bit of squinting at the screen wondering why everything was one pixel to the left), I finally got everything lined up.
